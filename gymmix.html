GymMix

GymMix is an app designed to empower users to craft personalized workouts from a curated library of exercises. Inspired by my own gym routine and the limitations of using basic checklists, I set out to create a more intentional, flexible, and motivating workout experience ‚Äî like Spotify, but for fitness.
