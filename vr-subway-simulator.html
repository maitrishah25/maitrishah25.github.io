Defining the Opportunity

To provide Outfront Media's advertiser partners with a practical solution to preview their ad creative for the subway in-car digital screens, our product experience team created a virtual reality simulator tool where customers could load and preview their creative in a realistic environment.

Approach

I architected the flows for the VR app flows and the playlist manager where advertisers would upload creative to preview in the VR experience.

Then partnering with our art director, we interpreted the brand as a 3D aesthetic for this particular context. We also collaborated on a storyboard to visualize key moments within the experience.

I also ran weekly critique sessions to review and provide feedback on design and development progress. This included reviewing the design of interactive 3D UI elements and 3D models by our in-house creative team, and the development of the overall experience by a VR development vendor. I brought in our key stakeholders as needed to ensure their alignment to the overall direction and progress of the project.
