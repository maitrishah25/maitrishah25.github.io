About Me

UX leader who makes ideas click — literally and figuratively!

Hi, I’m Maitri Shah! I’ve spent over a decade bringing clarity to complex design challenges and leading creative teams to deliver top-notch user experiences. I’ve had the privilege of working with brands like John Deere, Nike, and Arthur J. Gallagher — helping them streamline processes, boost revenue, and, of course, make users happy.

I’m passionate about combining strategy with creativity, whether it’s building design systems, refining user flows, or guiding cross-functional teams. My goal is always to make the digital world a little easier to navigate and a lot more enjoyable.

When I’m not deep in design, you’ll find me mentoring up-and-coming UX designers or exploring new ways to make products better for people. Let’s connect and see how we can bring some fun (and results!) to your next project.
