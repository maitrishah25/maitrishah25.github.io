Instructional Design as a Strategy

The Interactive Learning Center is home to the latest innovations in online legal education. These programs create immersive experiences to engage learners and motivate them to think critically and imaginatively to apply concepts to real-world scenarios.

Engagement is key. Real-world practical application is the goal.

Interactive Learning Center, Practising Law Institute

Behind-the-scenes creative talents such as visual designers, instructional designers, actors and video crews join forces to make the complex understandable and the abstract real. Formats are based on field research so the team can custom-tailor the experiences to learners' needs, and help them retain knowledge and broaden understanding and application of that knowledge.

About

Interactive Learning Center is PLI's home for the latest innovations in online legal education, challenge learners using "learning-by-doing" techniques in a variety of non-traditional, instructional formats.

About

Practising Law Institute is a nonprofit learning organization dedicated to keeping attorneys and other professionals at the forefront of knowledge and expertise. PLI is chartered by the Regents of the University of the State of New York, and was founded in 1933 by Harold P. Seligson.
