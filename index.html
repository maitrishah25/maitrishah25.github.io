Who I Am
and What I Do.

My name is Maitri, and my passion has always been to design and create. From designing in several mediums, whether it be websites, apps, graphics or even fashion, I've found the common thread to be having a brilliant idea and then doing everything it takes to bring it to life.

I love to think outside-the-box to create solutions that push the ball forward. It's deeply important to me to produce high quality work that makes a meaningful difference to others.
