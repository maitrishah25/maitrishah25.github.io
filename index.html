About

Imagining and creating has been an obsession, ever since I was little. It started with sketches on napkins as a child. My mom took notice and encouraged me to practice, and years later I found myself pursuing degrees in design and finance with plans of someday running a design studio.

The more I immersed myself further into the world of design, I also studied how to bring designs to life with coding. I realized that this is a medium where a person could really create anything they can imagine - a medium of the future, where artists could create masterpieces of a different form. And so I continue my journey, exploring UI, UX and product design, as filled with wonder and curiosity as ever...
