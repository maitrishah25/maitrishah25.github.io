Delivering Effective Presentations

Help participants in your program learn by aligning your presentation with the human cognitive architecture. Structure your slides to best help them understand and remember what you're teaching.

Using Questions

Avoid overloading learners and provide learning supports like repetition, and show them real-world relevance by questions. Questions are more effective to learning success.

Constructing Hypotheticals

It isn't enough to know the facts, you want learners to be able to get under the hood. That's why hypotheticals, if well-designed, can be valuable in triggering recall of what was learned and how to apply it.

Thought Leadership

Thought leaders are go-to people in their field of expertise. They move and inspire others with innovative ideas. They turn concepts into reality, and show how to replicate their success.
