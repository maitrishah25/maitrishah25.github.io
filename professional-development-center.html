Purpose and User Goals

The purpose of this site is to address the needs of a new customer base with the resources offered by our R & D team, mainly through video series focused on instructional design. The provided content has been reframed to a customer-centric perspective using the qualitative research gathered by product development. Since we had a good amount of videos, I wanted to craft the experience to feel immediately familiar. Where the video series and encompassing topics were not unlike episodes of shows.
