Payment: Do you accept insurance?

No, I am an out of network provider which means that you pay me directly. I will give you a bill at the end of each month, which you can send in for reimbursement from your insurance carrier. I often recommend that people choose an insurance carrier that has good reimbursement for out of network psychiatric care and that people get on a flexible spending plan, if not already on one.

This seems unfair: It is. We are in a sad state of affairs. While there are many laws about mental health parity, they are not often followed by insurance companies, and insurance companies often don’t reimburse by level of training. Insurance companies, often want to determine what kind of medical care a patient needs, by minimizing costs. I run my practice by making clinical decisions.
