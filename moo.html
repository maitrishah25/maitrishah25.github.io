Small Business Website

A client needed a website to establish her corporate identity online and provide a platform for customers to schedule music lessons with her.

Branding & Website Development

I worked with the client to understand how she desired to present her business to the world. Wanting a fresh, inviting and on-trend aesthetic, I created visuals and tailored a website style to bring it all together for her.

Photo Editing

The client had some photos for the site. I edited the photos to bring the visual style together for a cohesive look and feel.

Additional Functionality

The business being based on music lessons, I incorporated a way for customers to schedule time with the client throug the website.
