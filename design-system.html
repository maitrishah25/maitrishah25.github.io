Arthur J. Gallagher

Design System

Brought on as the leader of the very first in house UX design team at Gallagher, my role is to create, maintain and evangelize design systems that promote a more cohesive, accessible and modern user experience across digital touchpoints globally.

My first undertaking in that regard has been reimagining the digital experience for RPS, a company AJG recently acquired. Here are some highlights of the custom design system v1.0 that I created:

  • Recreated the type scale with robust and concise set of text styles, plus special classes for efficiently overriding color and weight as needed.
  • Included conventions to allow for more direct transfer between design and development.
  • Configured components to be dynamic, enabling design teams to quickly leverage into actual designs with actual content.
  • Structured for scalability by allowing design teams to stay synced with component updates as the design system evolves.
Framing the Problem
  • The previous onboarding experience was a confusing one, with all form fields for different types of users on the same screen. It was time-consuming and difficult to understand and complete.
  • No mobile view.
  • The branding, color palette and icons were not in accordance with the new brand guidelines.
