Private Practice

Psychiatry Services

The client was a psychiatrist who was looking to express her story and expand her private practice, which includes a clientele ranging from SNL actors to fashion models. The website explains experiencing psychiatry services like taking a road trip. I designed and built this website as a powerful way to reach a wider audience.

Framing the Problem

The client needed a way to prospect new clients and create leads. My first task was to explain the services in a way that's understandable to people - to connect the product and the market. I identified terms and concepts that are well-known in the field but wouldn't be transparent to potential clients immediately. These boiled down to psychotherapy, psychopharmacology and psychoanalysis.

At the same time, I had to think through the outcome we wanted from the user. Was it for the user to gain expertise in psychiatry? Not really. Ultimately, we wanted the user to make the connection between the services and their needs, and to set up an appointment.

Concept and Approach

We collaboratively envisioned that inviting the user on an informal exploration with my client as a cotraveller would take the complex concepts of psychiatry and deliver them as relatable, inviting and beneficial experience.
