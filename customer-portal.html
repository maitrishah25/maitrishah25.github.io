Defining the Opportunity

Gallagher needed to reduce customer support overhead. We solved for this by providing a self-service platform for customers to access and manage complex policy documents,

Approach

I architected the flows for the customer portal appication and regularly engaged 20+ executive stakeholders, successfully building alignment and forward movement on the project.

My team of designers fleshed out low fidelity wireframes that visualized information architecture and interactive functionalities. After stakeholder alignments and usability testing, my team brought the designs to high fidelity, polished with the Gallagher branded look and feel.

Along the way, I ran critiques with the design team to ensure consistency and alignment to overall goals. We leveraged the Gallagher design system that we created, and added updates based on new elements that we were commonly using for product design.
